I love shooting rocks! In nature, indoors, wherever the rocks are.

A little about me:

I am a solo freelance photographer with over 12 years of experience and hundreds of photo shoots behind me. Located in Lindon, Utah with a love for traveling and destination photoshoots.

I thrive on capturing your rocks, your story, your love. I want to provide you with images of stones so you never forget them. The tiny pebbles in your hands, the polished geodes, the love spoken through your collection as you present them to the world. Whatever your need is, I would be honored to be a part of it.

I am available for hire for any rock photography needs. Please contact me for more information.
