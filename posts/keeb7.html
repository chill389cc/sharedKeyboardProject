Black and Gray board
Anne Pro 2 with Gateron Browns, per-key RGB, Black

This is just my buildout of the Anne Pro 2. I took some pictures of it with both its stock keycap set and also some alphas that I stole from my friends Massdrop Alt. I also stuck an artisan that was given to me by a friend on the Esc, I think it looks pretty good, especially when you have Red RGB backlighting. Pretty cool! This board has per-key RGB and I got it for just $60 which was honestly such a good deal. Has a type-c connector too! Software sucks, beware.
