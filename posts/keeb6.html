Corsair K55 Mem-chanical 3-zone RGB Gamer Keyboard

This is just a mem-chanical keyboard that I bought. Thought I'd take some photos of me cleaning it and the finished, cleaned product. It's a nice board, but the RGB isn't fully addressable. Just three zones. The macros on the left side are kind of nice. I like mapping them to things like my email, etc. I'd recommend this, but it's nothing close to a mechanical board.
