Handmade AEKII 40%
Made from parts of an Apple Extended Keyboard II

This keyboard was super fun to make. I used the original plate, caps, switches, and various other parts of an old AEK II to make my first 40% and first hand-wired build. It has 41 switches, Alps SCKM Damped Clears with lubed springs, a centered Type-C port, and a hand-made plywood case. It all started when I bought an AEK II (Apple Extended Keyboard II, "made in the early 1990's" according to Deskthority. The board was pretty dusty and dirty but definitely not in terrible condition.
