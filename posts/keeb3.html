Laser-cut mini keyboard!
Handmade handwire 40% board with Zilent 67g and Whitefox

This keyboard was a year in the making. First I had to design the parts in Autocad, then I had to laser-cut each layer, sometimes having to re-cut them if I had to make a modification. Once it was done I got to handwire it and then screw it together! Super fun process. What do you guys think?
